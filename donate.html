Support Jack of Trades Donkey Rescue

Jack of Trades Donkey Rescue is funded entirely by donations and adoption fees, which help cover initial rescues, veterinary care (including castrations), farrier services, medications, hay and feed, shelter, and necessary equipment. Your support is crucial to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming these donkeys and miniature horses.

You can support us in several ways:

  • Donate via Venmo, PayPal, check, or by contacting us directly by email.
  • Purchase items directly from our Amazon Gift List.
  • Donate equine and farm-related materials (gates, lumber, tools, and supplies).
