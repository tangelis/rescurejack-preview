Welcome

Welcome to Jack of Trades Donkey Rescue, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit in Alpharetta, Georgia. We are dedicated to rescuing donkeys and miniature horses from stressful and challenging situations.

Our Mission

We rescue and rehabilitate donkeys and miniature horses from stressful and challenging situations, then match them with suitable and loving homes.

Important Note

We do not adopt out donkeys as protection or guardian animals.
