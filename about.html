About Us

We rescue and rehabilitate donkeys and miniature horses from stressful and challenging situations, then match them with suitable and loving homes.

Every rescue is a step toward giving these animals a second chance at a happy, fulfilling life.

Rescue

We bring donkeys and miniature horses out of stressful situations.

Rehabilitate

Daily care, vet services, feed, farrier support, and handling.

Rehome

We match animals with the most suitable and loving homes.
