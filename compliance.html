Georgia Charity Readiness Checklist

  • Register and keep current charitable solicitation status with the Georgia Secretary of State before broad public fundraising campaigns.
  • Display legal nonprofit identity, contact info, and 501(c)(3) status clearly on public pages.
  • Retain donation records and provide donor acknowledgments/receipts for tax purposes.
  • Maintain a privacy policy covering donor/contact form data handling and retention.
  • Maintain a terms and transparency page describing fundraising intent and governance.
  • Review annual federal and state filing deadlines (for example, IRS Form 990 cycle and Georgia renewal windows).
