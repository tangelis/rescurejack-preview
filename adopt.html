Donkeys Available for Adoption

Browse photos and bios of rescue animals looking for loving homes. We work hard to place each donkey and mini horse in a safe, suitable environment.

We do not adopt out donkeys as protection or guardian animals.

Donkeys are social and thrive with donkey companionship.
While many donkeys can get along with horses, each donkey's personality should guide placement.
Donkeys are playful and often do best with same-sex donkey companions.
A minimum of 1/2 acre of cleared land per donkey is recommended.
Shelter should be at least 10' x 10' per donkey.
We do not adopt out donkeys as protection or guardian animals.

Adoption Fee Information

We invest significant time, labor, and resources into every animal. Adoption fees help cover that care and ensure each donkey has the best chance in a new home.

  • Adoption fees help cover rescue intake costs.
  • Fees support veterinary care, including castration when needed.
  • Fees support feed, hay, and farrier services.
  • Fees support handling and training while animals are in rescue care.
  • Adoption fees can start around $800 for an uncastrated, unhandled jack.
  • Adoption fees for handled, castrated jacks or jennys can start around $1500.
  • Animals go to new homes with a halter and lead rope.
